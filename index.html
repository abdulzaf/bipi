Baseball Injury & Performance Initiative

Designed by elite baseball strength coaches, the Baseball Injury and Performance Initiative 10 (BIPI 10) is a free strength and power program that can be completed in 10 minutes per day, 5 days a week. It requires no additional training equipment. It is recommended for youth athletes and is suitable for all levels of play. BIPI 10 training is to be performed after practices, games, and throughout the year.
